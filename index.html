About

I am a Senior ML Software Engineer at Turing, where I work at the intersection of software engineering and modern AI systems. I contribute to the development of agentic platforms that automatically evaluate and verify agentic tasks. The Turing mission is to help frontier AI research labs and enterprises advance model capabilities.

I have 7 years of industry experience across a range of roles and domains. I began my career as a full-stack developer at Hinza Co, then joined Tapsell, Iran’s largest mobile advertising network handling over 50 million daily requests. At Tapsell, I worked as a software and data engineer before being promoted to a technical leadership role. Before Turing, I was a software engineer at Wave HQ, a financial platform serving over 2 million small business owners, where I worked on building robust backend systems for critical risk-assessment workflows.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science from the University of Calgary, completed under the supervision of Professor Robert Walker. My master’s thesis, "Graph Generalization in Software Engineering", focused on practical applications of novel graph-based algorithms for large-scale software repositories (GitHub and journal). I earned my BSc in Software Engineering from the University of Tehran.
